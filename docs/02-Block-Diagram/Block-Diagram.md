## Decision Making

This Block Diagram was constructed with the neccessary components required for a functioning hall effect sensor. Components are surface mounted to conserve space and meet neccesary requirements. LEDS are included for messaging and debugging signals. The microcontroller of choice is the 48 pin PIC18F57Q43, due to its multiple GPIO pin and easy programmability. The PIC will be programmed with the SNAP in programmer.
